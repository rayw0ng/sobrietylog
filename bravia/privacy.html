Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 18, 2025

1. Overview

Basic TV Remote is a fully local application. We do not collect, store, transmit, or access any personal data.

2. No Data Collection

The App does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage data
  • Analytics or tracking data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

The App does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.

3. Local Functionality Only

  • All remote-control communication happens directly between your iOS device and your TV on your local network.
  • PSK values, IP addresses, and settings are stored only on your device and are never uploaded or shared.

4. No Servers and No Online Storage

The App does not connect to developer-owned servers. No user data is uploaded, transmitted, or processed online.

5. Children’s Privacy

Because the App collects no data, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be made available within the App or App Store listing.

7. Contact
