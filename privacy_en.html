Privacy Policy

Sobriety Log ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data you create remains stored locally on your device.

2. Purchases and Free Trial

The App offers an optional in-app subscription to access its full functionality. Users are offered a one-month free trial to experience all features of the App. After the trial period, users will be charged according to the subscription plan they selected. Payment is securely handled through the App Store. The App itself does not process or store any payment information.

3. Data Security

Since all your data is stored only on your device, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your own device.

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Updates will be posted on this page.
