Privacy Policy

PlayFetch ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data you create remains stored locally on your device.

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase to unlock all features. Payment is securely processed through the App Store. The App itself does not process or store any payment information.

Since all your data is stored only on your device, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your own device.
